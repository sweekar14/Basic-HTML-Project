Pokhara University, Kaski, Nepal

Infrastructures

Pokhara University has started its operation from rented premises. Laying foundation stone of the central office of the university was held at Khudi of Dhungepatan in Ward No 12 of Lekhnath Municipality in 2000. The university has been launching of its all activities, both academic and administrative, from its own building here in Lekhanath by the year 2007. A spacious dining hall.


Altogether there are 13 academic and administrative offices including University Central Office, Office of University’s Service Commission, Office of the Controller of the Examinations, Contact Office in Kathmandu, Office of the Dean of Faculty of Management Studies, Office of the Dean of Faculty of Science and Technology, Office of the Dean of Facility of Humanities and Social Sciences, School of Business, School of Health and Allied Sciences, School of Engineering, School of Development and Social Engineering, Central Library, are into full-fledged operation.Pokhara University fully depends on government grants for financing the capital expenditure especially for infrastructure development. University has policy to mobilize the private sector and donors to develop the university infrastructure such as hostel, school building and residential facilities, and credit the infrastructure by the name of the concerned donor.Some academic building has built from matching grants of second higher education project under through by the University Grants Commission, Nepal. The Girls Hostel is Constructed by Indian Government.
